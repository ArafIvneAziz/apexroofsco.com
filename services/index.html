Frequently asked questions

How long does a roof installation take?

Most residential roof installations take 1-3 days, depending on your home's size and complexity. Commercial projects may require additional time. We'll provide a specific timeline during your free consultation.

How do I know if my roof needs repair or replacement?

Deciding whether your roof needs repair or a full replacement can be challenging, but certain signs can help guide the decision. Here’s what to look out for:

Visible leaks or water stains on ceilings
Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
Excessive granule loss in gutters
Sagging roof deck or structural issues
Roof age (most asphalt shingles last 20-25 years)

Our certified inspectors can provide a professional assessment and honest recommendations.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Yes! We offer emergency services 7 days a week from 7AM to 8PM. Our rapid-response team will quickly assess damage, implement temporary solutions if needed, and develop a permanent repair plan.

How much does a roof replacement cost?

Replacement costs vary based on size, materials, complexity, and current conditions. As a guideline, most Texas residential roofs range from $8,000-$20,000, but we provide detailed, transparent quotes after a thorough inspection. Financing options are available to make your new roof affordable.

Do you offer warranties on your roofing services?

Absolutely! As certified GAF and Owens Corning contractors, we provide manufacturer warranties on materials plus our own workmanship guarantee. We'll explain all warranty details during your consultation.
