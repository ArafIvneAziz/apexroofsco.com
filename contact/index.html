Frequently asked questions

What factors influence the price of a roofing project?

Several factors affect pricing, including your roof's size and pitch, material choices (including premium GAF or Owens Corning products), existing damage requiring repair, and any additional features like ventilation improvements. We provide detailed, transparent quotes after a thorough inspection.

How long does a roof installation take?

Most residential roof installations take 1-3 days to complete, depending on size, complexity, and weather conditions. Commercial projects may require additional time. Our team works efficiently while maintaining our quality standards, and we'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your roofing services?

Absolutely! As both a Certified GAF Roofing Contractor and Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, we offer manufacturer warranties on materials plus our own workmanship guarantee. These warranties provide long-term protection for your investment.

Do you provide emergency roofing services?

Yes! We offer emergency services throughout our Texas service areas, 7 days a week from 7AM to 8PM. Our experienced team will quickly assess storm damage, implement temporary solutions if needed, and develop a permanent repair plan.

Are there any hidden costs associated with your pricing plans?

No hidden costs, period. We provide transparent, detailed pricing with all fees clearly outlined before any work begins. And with flexible financing options available, we make quality roofing accessible to all Texas property owners.
