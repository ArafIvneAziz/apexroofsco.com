Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2025
Collecting Personal Information

At Apex Roofs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our website, request services, or communicate with our team.

We may collect personal information from you when you:

  • Request a quote or roofing consultation.
  • Schedule a service appointment.
  • Contact us via phone, email, or our website form.
  • Sign up for our newsletter or promotions.
  • Apply for financing options.
  • Submit an insurance claim form.

The types of information we collect may include:

  • Name, address, email address, and phone number.
  • Property information relevant to roofing services.
  • Payment and financing information.
  • Insurance information (when applicable).
  • Communication preferences.
Using Personal Information

We use the information we collect for legitimate business purposes, including:

Apex Roofs uses your personal information to provide and improve our roofing services across Texas. We process your information to prepare accurate quotes, schedule and perform roofing services, process payments, and communicate with you about your project. With your consent, we may also send promotional materials about special offers or new services that might interest you.

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Sharing Personal Information

We respect your privacy and limit sharing your information to specific circumstances:

  1. Service partners and contractors who assist in providing our roofing services.
  2. Payment processors and financing partners (when you choose financing options).
  3. Insurance companies (when processing claims with your authorization).
  4. Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Any third parties who receive your information are required to maintain its confidentiality and security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and review the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
