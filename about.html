About us

At Apex Roofs  we've been delivering exceptional roofing solutions across Texas for over 14 years with unwavering professionalism and integrity. As both a Certified GAF Roofing Contractor and Owens Corning Preferred Contractor, our expert team brings industry-leading credentials to every roof installation, repair, and inspection we perform.

Led by owner Dylan De Leon, we've built our reputation on doing things the right way—every time. We use only premium materials and cutting-edge techniques to create durable, long-lasting roofs that stand up to Texas's challenging weather conditions. From South Padre Island to San Antonio and Houston, property owners trust us when they need protection they can count on.

Whether you need residential or commercial roofing, emergency storm repairs, or help navigating insurance claims, our responsive team is ready to deliver exceptional craftsmanship with flexible financing options available.
