FAQs

How quickly can Apex Roofs respond to my roofing emergency?

As a local Texas roofing company operating 7 days a week from 7AM to 8PM, we provide same-day emergency response for storm damage and leaks. Our teams across South Texas ensure we can quickly protect your property when you need us most. We understand Texas weather doesn't follow a schedule - and neither do our emergency services.

What financing options does Apex Roofs offer?

We believe every Texas homeowner deserves a quality roof regardless of budget constraints. That's why Apex Roofs offers flexible financing options with competitive rates and payment plans tailored to your financial situation. Our financing process is straightforward, with quick approvals that allow your roofing project to begin promptly without straining your finances.

What roof types and materials does your certified team install?

As both GAF Certified and Owens Corning Preferred Contractors, we install premium asphalt shingles with industry-leading warranties. We also specialize in metal roofing systems perfect for Texas weather extremes, commercial flat roofing solutions, and impact-resistant options for storm-prone areas. All materials are selected for their proven performance in our harsh Texas climate.

How does Apex Roofs help with insurance claims after storm damage?

With 14+ years of experience handling Texas storm damage, our team expertly guides you through the entire insurance claim process. We provide thorough documentation, professional damage assessments, and direct communication with your insurance company. Our expertise in identifying all compensable damage ensures you receive the full coverage you're entitled to, making the repair process as stress-free as possible.
